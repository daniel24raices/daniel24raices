About Me

I am Daniel Raices. Born in 1993 in Caracas, Venezuela. Currently based in Madrid, Spain.

I am a professional translator, Spanish is my mother tongue and I also speak English, French, and Italian.

Recently, I have ventured into the world of front-end development. I am eager to absorb as much knowledge as possible and develop new skills. The transition from translation to coding may seem like a leap, but for me is a chance to merge my love for languages with my fascination for technology. The path ahead may be uncertain, but I am excited to embark on this journey of growth and discovery.
